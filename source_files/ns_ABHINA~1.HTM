        Abhinav Kumar joins Reaxys product team to lead Chemistry Solutions Strategy & Business Development

        Published by Ivan Krstic on 18 March 2020 15:23 Less than 1 min read

        I am very pleased to announce that on March 1st, 2020 Abhinav Kumar joined ELSS product team as Chemistry Solutions Strategy & Business Development manager, reporting to me in the Chemistry Solutions product group.

        In his new role Abhinav will be responsible for defining, supporting and executing chemistry solutions strategy and will be working closely with the rest of the product, ELSIP, PSG, marketing, sales and delivery team. In our journey of transforming Reaxys to become the leading chemistry information solution in the ecosystem, Abhinav will take the lead on:

        • Market analysis, refinement and validation as well as commercial development of future predictive offerings (Horizon 2 of business growth)

        • Scalability and commercialization of services offerings

        • Identifying synergies between LS and Health offerings

        Abhinav will be working with a cross-functional team to hypothesize, test, refine and validate the bets: assess and confirm market needs, competitive landscape, willingness to pay, identify potential risks and mitigation strategy, leading to a commercial revenue, risk sharing framework and legal agreement. These vital activities will ensure that our efforts are aligned to our commercial goals and ensure that we gain market share by delivering monetizable innovations.

        Abhinav holds a doctorate in Respiratory Medicine from King’s College London and brings three years of experience in Life Sciences strategy consulting at Monitor Deloitte. Abhinav builds upon his success in leading the Chemistry Solutions Marketing function where in the last 18 months he has had significant impact by delivering Reaxys Tableau Dashboard for Value Reinforcement, Sales Training and Enablement for Reaxys, business development and end-to-end delivery of go to market for Reaxys Predictive Retrosynthesis.

        Please join me in congratulating Abhinav on his new role.

        Best regards,

        Ivan Krstic, PhD

        Head of Chemistry Solutions | Director Product Management
