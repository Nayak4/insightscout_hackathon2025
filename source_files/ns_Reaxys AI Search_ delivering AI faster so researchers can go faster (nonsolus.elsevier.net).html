        Reaxys AI Search: delivering AI faster so researchers can go faster

        Published by Mathew Pitt-Bailey on 31 July 2025 10:11 2 min read

        We’re thrilled to announce that Corporate Markets has launched Reaxys AI Search—an AI-powered natural language document search that transforms how chemists and other researchers discover chemistry information. The solution allows users to ask questions in everyday language and get relevant, comprehensive results far more quickly and easily than before. 

        Why it matters

        • Traditional keyword searches can be complex and slow. 
        • Reaxys AI Search levels the playing field: faster, more relevant results for all users, regardless of expertise. 
        • Reaxys AI Search is the first chemistry database in the world to introduce natural language document search.
        • We’re moving fast—responding to customer feedback and launching early, with confidence—to stay ahead of competitors. 
        • This is just the beginning—future releases will include conversational chat, summarization, and full integration. 

        How does Reaxys AI Search work?

        • Uses advanced AI models to surface Reaxys’s trusted, curated content. 
        • Converts natural language queries into vectors, finding results based on scientific meaning, not just keywords. 
        • Combines semantic (meaning-based) and lexical (keyword) search for the most comprehensive answers. 
        • Handles complex language, spelling variations, abbreviations, and synonyms seamlessly. 
        • Results are transparent, relevant, and privacy-compliant—no data used to train external models. 

        Key benefits

        • Significantly improves relevancy and precision over current systems. 
        • Speeds up research, literature reviews, and decision-making—saving time and reducing missed insights. 
        • Supports a broad user base: chemists, materials scientists, pharma, biotech, and interdisciplinary researchers. 
        • Available now to all registered Reaxys users. 

        Our agile approach

        • Launched early to deliver immediate value and gather real-world feedback. 
        • Responding quickly to user needs—features like filters, summaries, and follow-up questions are already in development. 
        • We’re partnering with customers on continuous improvement, making sure we stay ahead of the competition. 

        Next steps

        • Explore Reaxys AI Search on our website today. 
        • Stay tuned for full integration, expanded features, and new capabilities in 2026.
        • Keep pushing the boundaries—faster, smarter, together. 

        Thanks to everyone who made this happen. Our shared commitment to understanding and supporting our customers has been at the heart of this successful collaboration.
